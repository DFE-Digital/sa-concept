Apply

When you start an application, you'll be able to see this in the applications list in the finance section of this service.

Progress through the application will be saved, and you don't have to complete it in one go.

We'll use information we already hold about your trust and schools wherever possible.

Supporting documents

You'll need to upload supporting documents as part of your application.

These could include:

  • quotes
  • plans and drawings from builders or architects
  • photographs and evidence
  • planning application documents

You can upload documents in any of the following formats:

  • PDF
  • JPEG
  • PNG
  • DOCX
  • XLSX
  • PPTX
  • ODT
  • ODS
  • ODP
  • CSV
  • ZIP

We check uploads for viruses and malware, however, you should also ensure the documents you upload are correct, free from viruses, and are relevant to the application.

Your application may be rejected if documents are missing, cannot be opened, or are the incorrect document.

Getting help and support

If you have any questions about the application process, please contact us, via messages at the top of the site.
