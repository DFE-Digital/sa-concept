Application guidance

Success rate is around 60% of all applications and the reasons for not being successful can include:

  • a larger than expected number of applications competing for limited funding
  • the quality of the applications
  • applications that don't align to the priorities
  • weak or little supporting evidence

It is important that you read the guidance and supporting information before you start your application.

Things to consider when completing your application

Demonstrating quality candidates

Clearly showing that there are pupils with exceptional digital skills and literacy.

Strong supporting evidence

Include robust evidence such as work experience feedback, exam or test results, and work demonstrating digital skills.

Clear and detailed application

Ensure your application is clear, well-structured, and comprehensive, covering the scope, objectives, and outcomes.
