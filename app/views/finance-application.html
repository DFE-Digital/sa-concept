What this funding is

The Department for Education (DfE) provides a grant to support setting up beehives on school premises.

The funding covers:

  • training up to 5 beekeepers with the National Bee Unit provided by the Animal and Plant Health Agency
  • nstallation of up to 3 beehives and provision of bee colony
  • membership of the National Beekeepers Association
