You asked:

How do I deal with complaints from parents?

This is what I have found:

Your complaint's policy can be found in the compliance section, under "Our complaints procedure"

Here is an overview of your policy:

  1. Acknowledge the complaint

    In writing or by email, within 5 working days

  2. Stage 1

    Investigate and provide a response within 10 working days

  3. Stage 2

    Complainant can escalate within 10 working days of the response

    Acknowledge the escalation within 5 working days

    Investigate and respond within 20 working days

  4. Stage 3

    Complainant can escalate within 10 working days of the response

    Acknowledge the escalation within 5 working days

    Governors and trustees investigate and respond within 20 working days

  5. Escalation to DfE

    Complainant can escalate within 10 working days of the response
